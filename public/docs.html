Introduction

LeetLink is an opinionated, minimalist roadmap platform designed to help software engineers master data structures and algorithms without the noise. We prioritize high-signal problem lists curated by difficulty, topic, and company.

How to Use

You don't need an account to start. Browse the roadmap on the homepage, select a track (like Beginner Patterns or By Company), and start solving.

Your progress is automatically saved to your browser's local storage. If you want to sync your progress across devices, simply click Sign in with Google in the top right corner.

Curated Roadmaps

We've broken down hundreds of problems into focused lists:

  • Beginner Patterns: Core fundamentals like Two Pointers and Sliding Window.
  • Advanced Algorithms: Graphs, Dynamic Programming, and Tries.
  • By Company: Targeted lists for Meta, Google, Amazon, and others.

Progress Tracking

Click the circular toggle next to any problem to mark it as complete. The progress counter at the top of the roadmap will update automatically.

If logged in, your data is securely stored in our backend, making sure you never lose track of your interview prep journey.

Video Resources

We believe the best way to learn is by watching experts break down the solution. That's why every problem in our database comes with a hand-picked YouTube tutorial link from top educators.
